our services

treadmill

Treadmill can be excellent equipment to help you train for your next marathon or your next big race.

yoga

Sun Salutation and Kapal Bhati pranayama are highly useful for losing weight. Moreover, with regular practice of yoga, we tend to become more sensitive to our body and its needs. This, in turn, helps keep a check on our food intake and body weight.

skipping

Skipping is a great cardio and an aerobic exercise. It helps to tone our calves, tighten our core, build stamina and improve our lung capacity.

Boxing can help improve our heart health, body composition, and strength, as well as lower our blood pressure and aid weight loss. Most of all, boxing is a fun way to get some exercise and release stress in a safe and healthy way.
